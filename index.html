About

Staff-scope data engineer based in London. Most recently at Meta (Messaging Integrity / Trust & Safety), building data infrastructure for a messaging platform serving billions of users. Before that: led analytics engineering teams at Career.io, Flip, IU Group (Syntea AI), and McMakler; started in data science at McKinsey.

Winner of the EMEA DE Festival Hackathon 2026 (Meta; London / Zurich / Tel Aviv / Dublin) with Untangler -- an AI-powered pipeline restructuring methodology now being integrated into Meta's internal tooling.

Outside of work, I organise and play competitive quiz games (What? Where? When?) across Europe -- 1,000+ tournaments played -- and build data tools for the community.
